That’s me, Marco.
I’m a 22 years old guy that loves Arts, Design and travelling around the world!
I came from a small city in the south Italy with amazing beaches, Lecce. Here I started doing some stuff in graphic design world only for a reason: when I was 15 years old I needed a flyer for an Hip-Hop event that I was organizing and I didn’t have enough money for a real graphic designer. So I downloaded Adobe Illustrator and I started to do very ugly flyers for my events (fortunately you will never find them on the web!). In the following years I founded an association dealing with arts and Hip-Hop culture, with a group of friends, and I used to produce stickers, t-shirts, flyers and some other stuff collaborating with my friends’ art: graffiti. After this period, I began to make some money (very few money) doing menus for restaurants, artwork for rappers, logos for friends’ projects.
One day, I met a professional and amazing graphic designer, which brings me in his communication agency (Big Sur) for one-year stage. Ok, at this point of my life, I really understood what graphic design is! Now, I’ve moved to Milan for studying Communication Design at Politecnico, and I’ve also started my freelance career.
That’s my story in very few words, I hope you didn’t get bored.
I also hope you like what I do, and if you are interested you can contact me through mail, Facebook, Behance, or with an pigeon carrier if you can!
